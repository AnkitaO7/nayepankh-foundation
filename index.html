About Us

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-led NGO dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, awareness campaigns, food drives, hygiene support, and social initiatives.

Started during the Covid pandemic, the organization continues to work towards building a safer, stronger, and more compassionate society for people and animals alike.
